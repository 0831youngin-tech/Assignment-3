Sukyoony
 Graphic Designer & Founder of Mohs
Bio:  I am a graphic designer working across books, branding, and objects. My work focuses on expressing emotions through color, form, and material.

Through Mohs, I explore everyday objects as a way to build my own visual language. I am interested in creating work that is both intuitive and lasting.

apple

A spatial logo application that enhances brand identity within a minimal retail environment.
The design focuses on clarity, hierarchy, and the seamless integration of branding into physical space.

kleenex

A playful yet structured packaging system that explores color, repetition, and modular composition.
The design balances brand recognition with a fresh visual rhythm to create shelf impact.

super matcha

A bold and cohesive visual system built through a limited color palette and strong typography.
The project translates brand identity into a range of lifestyle goods with consistency and energy.

bombay sapphire

A refined collaboration that highlights the brand’s iconic blue through product and material exploration.
The design emphasizes elegance while maintaining a contemporary and functional aesthetic.

OHUI

A minimal and emotional packaging design that focuses on balance, space, and subtle detail.
The use of restrained color and form enhances the premium identity of the brand.

THE HYUNDAI VIP GIFT

A delicate visual narrative expressed through illustration and composition.
The design aims to deliver a sense of intimacy and exclusivity through color and form.
